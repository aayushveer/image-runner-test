# Encryption
Encryption: PGP key available at https://www.imgrunner.com/.well-known/pgp-key.txt

# Our Commitment
We are committed to ensuring the security of our users' data. All image processing happens client-side in the browser. No files are uploaded to our servers.

# Reporting Security Issues
If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to us via email. We respond within 24-48 hours and work to resolve issues promptly.

# Scope
This policy applies to all Image Runner web applications and services.

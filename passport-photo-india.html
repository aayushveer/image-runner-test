Frequently Asked Questions

What is Indian passport photo size in mm?

Indian passport photo size is 35mm width x 45mm height (3.5cm x 4.5cm). This is the standard size accepted for Indian Passport, Aadhaar, PAN Card, Voter ID, and Driving License applications.

What is passport photo size in pixels for India?

At 300 DPI (print quality), Indian passport photo is 413 x 531 pixels. For digital uploads, Passport Seva accepts photos between 10KB-300KB in JPEG format.

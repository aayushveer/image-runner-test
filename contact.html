Frequently Asked Questions

Is Image Runner really free?

Yes! Image Runner is 100% free with no hidden costs, no premium tiers, and no watermarks. We keep it free through optional advertising.

Do you collect my images?

No! All processing happens in your browser. Your files never leave your device. We don't store, analyze, or share your images. Read our Privacy Policy

Can I use Image Runner for commercial purposes?

Yes! Image Runner is free for both personal and commercial use. Create product photos, marketing materials, documents — whatever you need.

How do I report a bug?

Click the bug report button (usually a 💬 icon) in the bottom-right corner of any tool page. It will capture the details we need to fix the issue quickly.

Can you add a specific tool I need?

Absolutely! We regularly add new tools based on user suggestions. Email us at contact@imgrunner.com with your tool idea.

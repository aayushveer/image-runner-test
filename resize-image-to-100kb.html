100KB Photo Specifications

A 100KB image provides excellent quality for most purposes:

  • Dimensions: Typically 300x400 to 400x500 pixels
  • Quality: Sharp, clear details visible
  • Print Size: Good for passport-size prints
  • Email: Fast to attach and send
  • Web: Quick loading on all devices

Tips for Professional Photos

When preparing photos for job applications:

  • Use formal attire (shirt/blazer for men, professional dress for women)
  • Neutral background (white, light gray, or light blue)
  • Good lighting on face, no harsh shadows
  • Direct eye contact with camera
  • Recent photo (within last 6 months)

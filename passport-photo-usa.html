What is US passport photo size in inches?

Can I wear glasses in US passport photo?

No, since November 2016, the US State Department does not allow glasses in passport photos, even prescription glasses. The only exception is if you have a signed doctor's statement that you cannot remove them for medical reasons.

Can I smile in my passport photo?

The State Department requires a "neutral facial expression" with both eyes open. A small, natural smile is acceptable, but avoid big smiles that show teeth or change your appearance significantly.

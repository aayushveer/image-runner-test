Why Grey Background for UK Photos?

Unlike most countries, the UK specifically requires a plain light grey or cream background. This is because:

  • Better contrast with facial features
  • Reduces glare and shadows
  • Makes it easier to verify photo authenticity
  • HMPO biometric systems are calibrated for this background

Frequently Asked Questions

What size is a UK passport photo?

UK passport photo size is 35mm wide x 45mm high. Your head (from chin to crown, not including hair) should measure between 29mm and 34mm. Eyes should be between 28mm and 36mm from the bottom of the photo.

What background colour for UK passport?

UK passport photos require a plain light grey or cream background. Pure white backgrounds are NOT accepted by HMPO (His Majesty's Passport Office). Our tool provides the correct grey background option.

Can I wear glasses in UK passport photo?

No, as of the 2024 rules, you cannot wear glasses in UK passport photos unless you have a medical condition that prevents removal. This applies to all types of glasses including clear prescription lenses.

Privacy Policy

Image Runner is built to keep your files private. Processing happens in your browser, not on our servers.

Files and content

Your files do not upload to Image Runner servers. All processing happens locally in your browser. We do not store, scan, or retain your documents or images.

Analytics and ads

We may use analytics and advertising services to understand tool usage and keep the service free. These services can collect device or browser data and may use cookies or similar technologies.

Information you provide

Image Runner does not require signups. If you contact us in the future, we will only use your message to respond to your request.

Changes

We may update this policy to reflect product changes. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

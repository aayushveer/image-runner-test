Why 720x720 for Facebook Profile?

Facebook recommends at least 720x720 pixels for profile pictures because:

  • Display Quality: Shows at 176x176 on desktop, 196x196 on mobile, but larger when clicked
  • Timeline Posts: Profile picture appears at various sizes in news feed
  • Comments: Your DP appears next to all your comments
  • Messenger: Used in chat and video calls
  • Future Updates: Facebook may increase display size

Tips for a Professional Facebook Profile Picture

  • Use a clear, recent photo of yourself (for personal accounts)
  • Face the camera with good lighting
  • Keep branding consistent (for business pages)
  • Remember circular display on some views - keep important parts centered
  • Avoid text that may be cut off in circular crop
  • Update your photo periodically to keep profile fresh

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Facebook profile picture size in pixels?

Facebook profile picture size is 720x720 pixels for best quality. Facebook displays it at different sizes (176x176 on desktop, 196x196 on mobile) but stores the full resolution for when users click to view larger.

What is Facebook cover photo size?

Facebook cover photo displays at 820x312 pixels on desktop and 640x360 on mobile. For best results across all devices, use 820x462 pixels as it covers both dimensions with minimal cropping.

Why is my Facebook profile picture blurry?

Facebook DPs appear blurry when the source image is too small (under 400 pixels) or heavily compressed. Use our tool with a high-quality photo to create a crisp 720x720 pixel profile picture.

How to set full photo as Facebook DP?

Can I make a Facebook page profile picture?

How to Resize an Image Online

  1. Upload — drop, paste or pick one or up to 50 images.
  2. Choose size — enter width and height in pixels, percent, cm or inches, or pick a one-click preset (Passport, Instagram, WhatsApp DP, Aadhaar, UPSC).
  3. Resize — click Resize Image. Optionally enable Target Size to hit an exact KB.
  4. Download — single images download directly; multiple images pack into a free ZIP. No watermark.

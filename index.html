Frequently Asked Questions

What size should a passport photo be?

Indian passport photos are 35x45 mm (about 413x531 px). US visa photos are 2x2 in (about 600x600 px). Set exact dimensions in the tool.

How do I reach 50KB or 100KB?

Resize to the right dimensions, then adjust the quality slider to control file size. Lower quality reduces file size.

Does resizing reduce quality?

Downscaling keeps images sharp. Keep quality at 90-100% for best results. Upscaling can soften details.

What size is required for government exam forms?

Many exam forms require 35x45 mm photos (413x531 px) with 10KB-200KB limits. Use the dimension settings and quality slider to meet requirements.
